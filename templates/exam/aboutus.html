About Examination System

The online Examination portal is a web-based application for technical evaluation. The online examination portal not only replaces paperwork but also releases the workload of faculty. Most of the e-examination (Online Examinations) have fixed number of questions without randomizations, they have pool scalability. Online examination system is increasing rapidly with the change in scenario due to the pandemic, also it seems to be an easy, flexible, and secure methodology to carry the examinations effectively.
